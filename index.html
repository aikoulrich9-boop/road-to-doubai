ABOUT THE FORUM

The Strategic Bridge Between West Africa & the Gulf

The IVOIRE-DUBAI INVESTMENT FORUM 2026 marks the 3rd edition of a must-attend event between the West African business community and the Dubai Chambers. For three days, Abidjan welcomes a delegation led by Mr. Cyril Darkwa, Representative of the Dubai Chambers for West Africa, for a program structured around two complementary objectives:

Showcase Dubai's opportunities

Introduce Ivorian and West African entrepreneurs, investors and businesses to concrete opportunities for establishment, investment and commercial development in Dubai and the United Arab Emirates.

Identify concrete partnerships

Visit key Ivorian businesses and institutions, sector by sector, to detect partnership opportunities, investment flows, and market entry strategies between Côte d'Ivoire and the UAE.

100 Ivorian companies, selected across the country's main economic sectors, will participate in this forum.

EXECUTIVE ADVANTAGES

Bilateral Economic Catalysts

Understand why this summit connects two of the world's most dynamic and complementary economic ecosystems.

Why Dubai?

The Global Capital Hub

  • 100% Foreign Ownership Full operational ownership in free zones with zero capital repatriation limits.
  • Tax Advantages & Free Capital Flows Extremely competitive corporate tax structure and zero personal income taxation.
  • Global Logistics & Connectivity World-class aviation and maritime hubs connecting Europe, Asia, and Africa.
  • Innovation Ecosystem Gateway to global AI hubs, venture funds, fintech sandboxes, and web3 technologies.

Why Côte d'Ivoire?

The Gateway to West Africa

  • Fastest Growing African Economy Consistent double-digit infrastructure growth and political macroeconomic stability.
  • Gateway to the UEMOA Region A single market of over 120 million consumers utilizing the stable currency system.
  • Deepwater Ports & Logistics Expansion Major strategic ports in Abidjan and San Pedro serving landlocked West African nations.
  • Untapped Industrial Sectors Vast investment potentials in Agriculture, Energy, Mining, Tech, and Tourism.
